Vacancy and Credit Loss in real estate investing is the amount of money or percentage of net operating income that is estimated to not be realized due to non-payment of rents and vacant units.

Vacancy and Credit Loss = GSI x VCL

Where:

  • GSI is the total Gross Scheduled Income
  • VCL is the estimated percentage of Vacancy and Credit Loss

Generally, a percentage that is comparable to similar properties' actual experience is used to estimate the vacancy and credit loss for a subject property.
