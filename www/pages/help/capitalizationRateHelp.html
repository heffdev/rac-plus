Capitalization Rate (Cap Rate) is the ratio between the net operating income produced by a property and its capital cost or alternatively its current market value.

Cap Rate = NOI / V

Where:

  • NOI is the Net Operating Income
  • V is the Market Value, or the amount paid to obtain the asset

Alternatively, you can transpose the formula to solve for any of the variables:

V = NOI / Cap Rate

NOI = V x Cap Rate
